EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is made effective as of January 1, 2026, by and between:

EMPLOYER: Global Tech Solutions Inc. ("Company")
Address: 1000 Corporate Boulevard, Suite 500, New York, NY 10001

EMPLOYEE: David Williams ("Employee")
Address: 456 Park Avenue, Apt 12B, New York, NY 10022

1. POSITION AND DUTIES
The Company hereby employs the Employee as Senior Software Engineer.
The Employee shall report to the VP of Engineering and perform duties including:
- Lead development of core platform features
- Mentor junior developers
- Participate in architectural decisions
- Conduct code reviews
- Collaborate with product and design teams

2. COMPENSATION
Base Salary: $165,000 per year, paid bi-weekly
Signing Bonus: $15,000, payable within 30 days of start date
Annual Bonus: Up to 20% of base salary, based on performance

3. BENEFITS
The Employee shall be entitled to:
- Health, dental, and vision insurance (Company pays 80% of premiums)
- 401(k) retirement plan with 4% company match
- 20 days paid time off per year
- 10 paid holidays
- Life insurance (2x annual salary)
- Professional development budget: $3,000/year

4. EQUITY
Stock Options: 10,000 shares of Company common stock
Vesting Schedule: 4-year vesting with 1-year cliff
Exercise Price: Fair market value at grant date

5. WORK LOCATION
Primary Location: Company headquarters in New York, NY
Remote Work: Hybrid schedule (3 days in office, 2 days remote)

6. TERM OF EMPLOYMENT
This is an at-will employment agreement. Either party may terminate the employment relationship at any time, with or without cause, upon 2 weeks written notice.

7. SEVERANCE
If the Company terminates the Employee without cause:
- 3 months base salary continuation
- COBRA coverage for 3 months
- Accelerated vesting of 25% of unvested options

8. CONFIDENTIALITY
The Employee agrees to maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary information during and after employment. This obligation survives termination indefinitely.

9. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All inventions, discoveries, and work product created during employment shall be the exclusive property of the Company. The Employee hereby assigns all rights to such intellectual property to the Company.

10. NON-COMPETE
The Employee agrees not to work for any direct competitor of the Company for a period of 12 months following termination of employment, within a 50-mile radius of any Company office.

11. NON-SOLICITATION
For 18 months following termination, the Employee shall not:
- Solicit any Company employees to leave their employment
- Solicit any Company customers or clients

12. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Any disputes arising under this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration in New York, NY, in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association.

13. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York.

14. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, and agreements.

15. AMENDMENTS
This Agreement may only be modified by a written amendment signed by both parties.
